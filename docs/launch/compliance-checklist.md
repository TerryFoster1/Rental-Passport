# Compliance Checklist

## Privacy and Data Protection

- PIPEDA review required.
- GDPR readiness for export and deletion requests required.
- Consent logging implemented at foundation level.
- Document retention policy requires legal approval.

## Communications

- CASL review required before email campaigns or transactional email expansion.
- Resend configuration must use approved sender domains.

## Verification Consent

- Identity authorization requires legal review.
- Credit authorization requires legal review.
- Employment authorization requires legal review.
- Reference authorization requires legal review.

## Housing Compliance

- Regional housing compliance review required before broad launch.
- No applicant scoring, ranking, or approval recommendation is implemented.
