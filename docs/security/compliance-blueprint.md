# Compliance Blueprint

This blueprint identifies compliance requirements and areas requiring legal review before launch.

## PIPEDA

Planning requirements:

- Consent for collection, use, and disclosure
- Purpose limitation
- Data minimization
- Access requests
- Correction requests
- Safeguards for sensitive information
- Vendor and provider accountability

Legal review required.

## GDPR Readiness

Planning requirements:

- Lawful basis mapping
- Data subject access requests
- Deletion requests
- Data export requests
- Retention schedules
- Processor/subprocessor tracking
- Cross-border transfer review

Legal review required.

## CASL

Secure invitations, verification outreach, reference emails, employer emails, and notification preferences must be reviewed for CASL compliance.

Legal review required.

## Credit Authorization

Credit review requires explicit authorization, jurisdiction-specific disclosures, provider rules, and retention limits.

Legal review required.

## Employment Authorization

Employer contact and employment verification require tenant consent, clear purpose, and secure response collection.

Legal review required.

## Reference Authorization

Reference outreach requires tenant consent, purpose limitation, and careful storage of responses.

Legal review required.

## Identity Verification Consent

Government ID and selfie handling require explicit consent, secure storage, retention limits, and deletion process.

Legal review required.

## Document Retention

Retention policy must define document type, purpose, minimum retention, maximum retention, deletion triggers, legal holds, and audit requirements.

Legal review required.

## Deletion Requests

Deletion workflow must handle user data, documents, audit obligations, legal holds, and downstream provider data.

Legal review required.

## Access Requests

Users should be able to request their stored profile, documents, activity, verification records, and consent history subject to legal limits.

Legal review required.

## Regional Housing Compliance

Forms, filters, questions, screening criteria, lease templates, and landlord views must adapt by jurisdiction. Illegal screening filters must not appear.

Legal review required by jurisdiction.

## Regional Lease Requirements

Lease templates and electronic signature workflows require jurisdiction-specific review.

Legal review required.

## Escrow And Verified Deposit

Verified Deposit may involve trust accounting, escrow licensing, consumer protection, and regional rent/deposit laws.

Legal review required before any implementation.
