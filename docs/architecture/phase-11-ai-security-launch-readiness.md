# Phase 11 - AI Assistance, Security Hardening, and MVP Launch Readiness

Phase 11 prepares Rental Passport for controlled MVP launch.

## Implemented

- AI assistance helpers for landlord factual summaries and internal reviewer summaries.
- AI guardrails: no scoring, no approval recommendation, no rejection recommendation.
- Security controls registry.
- Monitoring signal registry.
- Audit coverage registry.
- Vercel security headers.
- Vite manual chunk configuration.
- Launch readiness documentation.

## AI Boundary

AI assists people. It explains facts, highlights outstanding items, and suggests review prompts. Humans make all verification, landlord, and rental decisions.

## Launch Status

Ready for controlled MVP launch after manual production verification, legal review, and security review. Not ready for broad public launch until penetration testing, monitoring, and operational drills are complete.
